Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Merge Kingdom ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve the game experience:

  • Device identifier — a randomly generated anonymous ID stored on your device, used to save your progress and enable leaderboards.
  • Gameplay data — in-game progress, scores, and event participation, stored on our servers to sync your game state.
  • Analytics events — anonymous usage data (e.g., screens viewed, features used) to help us improve the game. No personally identifiable information is included.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal contact information.
  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third-party data brokers.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — for in-app purchases and subscriptions. Purchases are processed by Apple and governed by their privacy policy.
  • Ad networks — we may show optional rewarded ads. Ad partners may collect device-level identifiers subject to your device's ad tracking preferences.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your gameplay data is stored securely on servers hosted by reputable cloud providers. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and follow security best practices to protect your data.

5. Children's Privacy

Merge Kingdom is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we only collect anonymous device identifiers and gameplay data, no personal information is gathered from any user regardless of age.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request deletion of your game data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of analytics by disabling analytics in your device settings.
  • Control ad tracking through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking on iOS).

7. Data Retention

We retain gameplay data for as long as you have the app installed and actively play. Inactive accounts may be deleted after 12 months of inactivity.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact us at: privacy@hakob17.com
